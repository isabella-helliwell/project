First let us explain briefly what the 8 features measure.For each feature there is a numerical range to be chosen from, High Life Ladder Score means a happier nation

The world happiness indicators are shown below. You can hover on each feature and get an explaination to what they are:

Log GDP per capita

Gross Domestic Product (between 6-12)

Life Expectancy at birth

Freedom to make life choises

Generosity

Perception of corruption

In goverment and business (between 0-1)


Positive affect

Are you feeling positive and happy often? (0-1)


Negative affect

Are you feeling negative and sad often? (0-1)
